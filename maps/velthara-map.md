## Overview

The Vel'thara world is not a planet. It is a resonance plane — a dimension where physical reality is organized by frequency rather than matter. "Geography" is harmonic structure. Mountains are sustained bass chords. Rivers are flowing melodies. Cities are symphonies. The ground is crystallized sound — a translucent surface rippling with visible wave patterns.

The plane contains 12 distinct civilizations ("harmonies") united in a resonance federation. Each harmony occupies a frequency band and a corresponding spatial region. They communicate through music. They experience the world as sound.

The ward boundary appears from their side as a vast silent wall — a frequency void. The Anchor Point aperture is the only opening.
