## Regions

### The River Valleys (East Coast) — Ahm-vel Territory
- **Terrain:** Temperate forest, river deltas, flood plains
- **Population:** ~120,000 River Folk across dozens of stilt-villages
- **Key settlement:** Harbor 1 (Ahm-toh river mouth, ~2,000, Elder Saya)
- **Rivers:** Ahm-toh (Sky River, largest), plus 6 named tributaries
- **Economy:** Fishing, flood-plain farming, reed-craft, river trade
- **Dawnwatch presence:** Stilt-home on south bluff, 300 yards from Harbor 1

### The Green Table (Central Plains) — Toh-vel Territory
- **Terrain:** Vast open plains, tall grass, scattered groves
- **Population:** ~100,000 Tall Ones in farming communes
- **Key feature:** Rememberer Heartland (central, largest commune cluster)
- **Rivers:** Ahm-ren (Earth River) runs along southern edge
- **Economy:** Agriculture (grain, root crops, herd animals), oral history preservation
- **Access from Harbor 1:** ~150 miles upriver on Ahm-toh, then overland west. 8–10 days.

### The Deep Forest (Interior) — KAH-SOL TERRITORY
- **Terrain:** Dense ancient forest, canopy 200+ ft, interior between Green Table and Singing Peaks
- **Population:** Unknown. The kah-sol. Canopy-dwelling, intelligent, predatory.
- **Key feature:** Ahm-kah (Forest River) enters from the west. No one follows it past the boundary.
- **Status:** AVOIDED by all five races except the Deep Ones. 2–3 people taken per year. 40+ from River Folk alone over 20 years. More active in autumn/winter.
- **Distance from Harbor 1:** ~200 miles west
- **⚠ DO NOT ENTER without full party and intelligence.**

### The Singing Peaks (West-Central Mountains) — Ren-vel Territory
- **Terrain:** Mountain range, 15,000+ ft peaks, high valleys, deep caves
- **Population:** ~40,000 Stone Singers in underground cities
- **Key feature:** Passes sealed against kah-sol (~200 years ago). Resonance Elders govern.
- **Economy:** Carved stone, refined metals, resonance crystals (raw). Buy food and textiles.
- **Access:** Mountain-foot trade markets (twice yearly). Passes sealed — entry by invitation only.
- **Intelligence priority:** They know what the kah-sol is. They will not say.

### The Running Grass (Northern Plains) — Kah-vel Territory
- **Terrain:** Northern steppe, seasonal frost, endless grassland
- **Population:** ~30,000 Wind Runners, nomadic
- **Key feature:** Follow elk-horse herds in annual circuit. No permanent settlements.
- **Economy:** Hides, bone, meat. Trade with all races during seasonal passes.
- **Access:** Seasonal — they pass through River Folk territory. Friendly, impermanent.

### The Drowned Coast (South/Southwest) — Sol-vel Territory
- **Terrain:** Rocky coast, sea caves, tidal flats, subterranean networks
- **Population:** ~10,000 Deep Ones (estimated — true number unknown)
- **Key feature:** Harbor 3 nearby. They enter the Deep Forest and return. Annual silent trade.
- **Economy:** Deep-sea shells, bioluminescent coral, unidentified metals
- **Access:** Harbor 3 (rocky, exposed). Or wait for annual trade visit at southern river-mouths.
- **Intelligence priority:** They survive the kah-sol. They know something.
