## Overview

The Underdark beneath the Hearthcoast is poorly documented on the surface. Pre-Sundering records (Archive, Vigil Field Manual) reference deep structures, but most surface-dwellers have no direct knowledge. The Dawnwatch's investigation draws on: Vaelith's ancient memory, Sylvara's Archive research, Torven's geological analysis, Seren's western coast observations, Zaryth's personal knowledge as a Drow exile, and field intelligence from the inter-city network.

**Key discovery (Day 10, 1249 AS — Aelindra Corridor 3 map room):** The 9 Binding-era anchor stations are connected by an underground tunnel network — the **Deep Road system**. This is not a single tunnel but a branching network linking all 9 stations through the Underdark. At the center of the network, deeper than everything else: an unmarked point — the **source** of the Binding frequency, located beneath the Old Foundation (Fenhollow Marshes, 540+ ft depth).

**Key principle (Vaelith):** "The Underdark is not a place. It is a *layer*. Everywhere you stand, it is beneath you. The question is only where the doors are."

## Zaryth's Personal Knowledge (Drow)

Zaryth Vel'quinar was raised in the Underdark before serving on the surface. His knowledge:

- **House Vel'quinar's territory:** Location unknown relative to surface geography. Zaryth traveled to the surface through a series of ascending tunnels over several weeks. He did not map the route — he was fleeing.
- **General Underdark structure:** Layered. The upper Underdark (0–3 miles deep) connects to surface caves, mines, and sinkholes. The middle Underdark (3–10 miles) contains the major Drow cities, fungal forests, and underground seas. The deep Underdark (10+ miles) is largely unknown even to Drow.
- **Navigation:** Drow use resonance markers, fungal bioluminescence patterns, and air current mapping. Zaryth retains these skills.
- **Hazards:** Darkness (total beyond entrance zones), hostile fauna (cave fishers, ropers, oozes, worse), rival Drow houses, mind flayer territories, fungal toxins, unstable geology, flooding.
- **The fallen house:** Zaryth does not know if House Vel'quinar still exists. The signet ring is broken — which in Drow culture may signify the house's destruction, or Zaryth's personal exile, or both.
