## Regions

### Hilagang Kapuluan (Northern Isles) — Voyager Territory
- **Terrain:** Small coral atolls, scattered, open water between
- **Population:** ~12,000 Voyagers (Mga Manlalayag)
- **Key figure:** Kapitan Habagat (82, master navigator, anchor voice)
- **Character:** Traders, sailors, open-ocean navigators. Most outward-facing. Gateway to the north.
- **Planting:** W-K3 "Voyager's Star" ✅ (Tala activated)

### Gitna ng Dagat (Heart of the Sea) — Coral-Singer / Rice Builder / Babaylan Territory
- **Terrain:** Large volcanic islands with coral platforms, rice terraces, deep harbors
- **Population:** ~25,000 (Coral-Singers ~15,000, Rice Builders ~10,000, Babaylan ~2,000 dispersed)
- **Key city:** Dagat-Puso (Heart of the Sea) — coral city, Datu Malaya, Dawnwatch Embassy
- **Key figures:** Datu Malaya (chief), Lakan-Diwa (senior Babaylan), Guro council (12 masters)
- **Character:** Cultural and political center. Coral cities, rice terraces, the Datu council convenes here.
- **Plantings:** W-K1 "Reef's Heart" ✅, W-K2 "Singer's Reef" ✅ (Guro Alon independent)

### Timog Dulo (Southern Edge) — Deep Fisher Territory
- **Terrain:** Outer islands, deep water, near the trench. Wilder, less settled.
- **Population:** ~13,000 Deep Fishers (Mga Mangingisda ng Kailaliman)
- **Key figure:** Bulan (lead diver, Nhilara's kapatid)
- **Character:** Frontier. Deep-water fishing, pearl diving. Toughest people in Kapuluan. Live closest to the Bakunawa.
- **Planting:** W-K4 "Diver's Light" ✅ (Bulan activated)

### The Deep Trench — Bakunawa's Domain
- **Depth:** 4,000 ft
- **Status:** HARMONIZED. Bakunawa sleeping. Root frequency integrated. Bridge holds.
- **Hazard:** Siyokoy (seasonal deep-water predators, now reduced after Dawnwatch intervention)
