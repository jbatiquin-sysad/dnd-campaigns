## CITIES

### Duskport
Mid-coast harbor city and the party's base. Seat of Magistrate Voss's inquiry. Home to the Temple of the Balanced Scale, harbor district, Scales District, Ashward (working-class), and council chambers on the hill.

- Population: ~12,000
- Connected by sea to: Veldmere (south), Salthollow (northeast coast)
- Connected by road to: Harrowgate (inland), Fenhollow (northwest)

### Aelindra
Northern cliff city and home of the Grand Archive. Seat of interim Archivist-General Veyra Solund. Built into a cliff face in four tiers from harbor to Crown. Politically sovereign.

- Population: ~8,000 (mostly scholars and Archive staff)
- Distance from Duskport: 4 days by ship (north along coast)
- Notable power: The Sealed Quorum

### Salthollow
Northeastern coastal university town. Home of the Athenaeum (Lorekeeper Idris Vane's private archive). Smaller than Duskport; no walls, no garrison, centered around the university.

- Population: ~5,000
- Links: Aelindra via Archivists' Road (2 days overland)
- Links: Duskport via Scholars' Coastal Road (3 days) or sea (2 days)

### Harrowgate
Inland trade city at the crossroads of the King's Road and the Mirren River. Seat of the Coinwright's Guild (Guildmaster Orvyn Tetch). Largest market in the region.

- Population: ~15,000
- Distance from Duskport: 3 days overland via King's Road
- Note: Financial heart of the region; no coast access

### Greymoor
Northern mining city in the Greypeak Mountains. Seat of the Pallid Court. Known for iron, silver, and deep-shaft mining. Cold, industrial, isolated.

- Population: ~7,000
- Access: Iron Road from Harrowgate/Duskport (5 days, rough terrain)
- Note: Deep mines predate the Sundering; some shafts are sealed

### Fenhollow
Northwestern marshland settlement and base of the Thornwatch (Commander Brecca Ashford). Not a true city, but raised villages linked by boardwalks and causeways through the marshes.

- Population: ~3,000 (distributed)
- Access: 2 days south from Greymoor through Thornveil Forest
- Access: 3 days northwest from Harrowgate

### Port Veldmere
Southern port city across the Hearthcoast Sea from Duskport. Seat of Marchion Drael Sulwick's trade empire. Controls southern sea routes.

- Population: ~18,000
- Distance from Duskport: 3 days by ship (south)
- Note: Larger harbor than Duskport; heavier traffic, less oversight
- Story hook: Calder's intended escape destination

## SEAS & WATERWAYS

### The Hearthcoast Sea
The central body of water connecting Duskport, Veldmere, Salthollow, and Aelindra. Busy shipping lanes; Sulwick's fleet dominates southern routes.

### The Mirren River
Flows from Greypeak foothills southeast through Harrowgate and toward the coast east of Duskport. Primary inland trade artery; Tetch's guild controls exchange rates at river ports.

### The Fenhollow Marshes
Vast wetlands northwest of Harrowgate. Tidal and treacherous; navigable mainly by locals and Thornwatch guides. Three sealed pre-Sundering ruins appear at low tide.

---

## FORESTS & WILDERNESS

### Thornveil Forest
Dense old-growth forest between Greymoor and Fenhollow. The Iron Road skirts its eastern edge. Interior is largely unmapped; locals avoid it.

### The Greypeak Mountains
Northern mountain range. Greymoor sits in a southern valley. Greypeak Pass is the only reliable crossing to lands beyond (largely unknown to the party).

## ROADS & ROUTES

### King's Road
Main overland artery from Duskport north through Harrowgate and onward toward Greymoor.

- 3 days: Duskport → Harrowgate
- 5 days: Duskport → Greymoor

### Iron Road
Branches from King's Road at Harrowgate and climbs into the Greypeaks toward Greymoor. Rough, steep, wagon-accessible, and slow; ore caravans use it constantly.

### Archivists' Road
Coastal route connecting Aelindra to Salthollow.

- 2 days overland
- Well-maintained by Archive funding

### Scholars' Coastal Road
Connects Salthollow south to Duskport along the northeastern coast.

- 3 days overland
- Less maintained than Archivists' Road
- Fishing villages along route

## LANDMARKS & NOTABLE LOCATIONS

### Dawnwatch Homestead (Fenhollow) ⌂
Four-acre homestead at the edge of Fenhollow. Purchased by the Dawnwatch after dismantling the Unchained Compact. Includes main house, alchemy shed, goat pen, herb garden, orchard, and north meadow (school site under construction). Caretaker: Tessa Marsh, with Ren and Lila.

### Tidemeet ⚓
River mouth where the Fenhollow river meets the Hearthcoast Sea. Sandy beach on the south bank, rocky tide pools on the north. Half-day walk south of Fenhollow (2 hours by cart). Coastal vessels dock here — sea route to Aelindra (3–4 days).

### Thornfield Dispatch 🏨
Coaching inn and courier service on the Thornfield Road, north of Fenhollow. Operated by Harlen Grubb. Used as the logistics channel for the Watcher's necromantic operation — dead drops, payments, and correspondence. Grubb cooperated with the Dawnwatch. The courier route is now compromised.

### Temple of Quiet Passage / Ashenmere ⛪
Ruined temple of the Order of the Ashen Veil. Located in Ashenmere Valley, hills east of Greymoor (3 days' walk). Destroyed by Veradis Kael's necromantic attack — 17 members of the Order killed. Site sealed by Greymoor patrol. A sealed vault in the lower catacombs remains unopened and unexplored.

### Barrowfen ☒
Small hamlet on the Thornfield Road, half-day north of Fenhollow. Site of Veradis Kael's catalyst field tests — 5 graves disturbed, 3 zombies raised and subsequently destroyed by the Dawnwatch. Village cemetery now quiet.

### Barrow of Keth-Varos 🔒
Pre-settlement ossuary barrow on a treeless hilltop northeast of Barrowfen. ~300 ancient remains. Ringed by standing stones. Catalyst was never applied — barrow confirmed inert. Entrance collapsed and sealed by the Dawnwatch with Arcane Lock. First barrow secured.

### Barrow of Vel Tharis 🔒
Pre-settlement barrow complex on coastal cliffs north of Aelindra. ~400–500 ancient remains. Sea cave entrance (accessible at low tide only, 6-hour window). Standing stone with closed eye symbol on the cliff top near Node 9. Second operative intercepted here before catalyst application. Entrance collapsed and sealed by the Dawnwatch with Arcane Lock. Second barrow secured.

### Greyveil Pass
Northern mountain pass beyond the Greypeaks. Location of outer ward Node 12. Site of the Watcher's requested meeting on the 1st of Highsun, 1247 AS. Largely unknown territory — beyond the party's current explored range.

### Greypeak Pass ☆
Only reliable mountain crossing. Controlled by Greymoor; strategic chokepoint.

### The Undercroft (Duskport)
Pre-Sundering underground complex beneath the city. Repository opened by the party in Session 1. Hollow Court origin. Now known to Voss's inquiry.

### The Three Sealed Ruins (Fenhollow Marshes)
Visible at low tide. Stone structures pre-dating current settlements; purpose unknown. Thornwatch blocks access. The Unchained Compact would want them opened.

### The Grand Archive (Aelindra)
Four tiers. The Crown summit holds restricted vaults. Sub-level 2 (Thessara's chamber) is secured. The Sealed Quorum operates from the Crown governance wing. Sylvara Dawnwhisper holds permanent Level 3 restricted access.

### The Athenaeum (Salthollow)
Idris Vane's private archive. University-adjacent but independently operated. Holds suppressed pre-Sundering documents. No military protection.

### The Coinwright's Guildhall (Harrowgate)
Tetch's seat of power. Controls regional currency exchange. River-facing and heavily guarded by private security.

## OUTER WARD NETWORK

The continental ward system has two layers:

**Inner Ward (The Root):** Renewed by the Dawnwatch (Session 3). Active. Healthy. Protects the heartland.

**Outer Ward:** 14 anchor nodes forming a perimeter around the continent. Pre-Sundering construction. Maintained by the Vigil of the Closed Eye until the Sundering. No maintenance in 1,247 years. Cascade in progress.

### Known Nodes

| Node | Location | Status | Notes |
|------|----------|--------|-------|
| **Node 9** | Coastal cliffs north of Aelindra (Vel Tharis) | **40%** — degrading | Measured by Sylvara with Ward Compass. Standing stone with closed eye symbol. |
| **Node 10** | Northeastern coast | **31%** — degrading | Reported by the Watcher. Not yet visited. |
| **Node 11** | Northern wastes (Sethrak's Threshold) | **FAILED ✖** | Confirmed failed by the Watcher. Cascade origin point. |
| **Node 12** | Greyveil Pass, northern mountains | **28%** — critical | Reported by the Watcher. Meeting site: 1st of Highsun. Most urgent node. |
| Nodes 1–8, 13–14 | Unknown locations | Unknown | Sylvara researching in Aelindra Archive. |

### Cascade Model
When a node fails, adjacent nodes absorb the load. Node 11's failure is stressing Nodes 10 and 12. At current decay rates:
- **Node 12 fails:** ~2 months (estimated mid-Goldmere 1247 AS)
- **Node 9 fails:** ~4–5 months (estimated Duskmere 1247 AS)
- **Full perimeter collapse:** 6–8 months if unchecked
