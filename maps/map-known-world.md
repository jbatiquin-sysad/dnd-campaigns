## CITIES

### Duskport
Mid-coast harbor city. Seat of Magistrate Voss's inquiry. Home to the Temple of the Balanced Scale, harbor district, Scales District, Ashward (working-class), and council chambers on the hill.

- Population: ~12,000
- From Homestead: 3 days overland (via Fenhollow → Harrowgate → King's Road), or 2 hrs to Tidemeet + 2 days by ship

### Aelindra
Northern cliff city and home of the Grand Archive. Seat of interim Archivist-General Veyra Solund. Built into a cliff face in four tiers from harbor to Crown. Politically sovereign. 🕳 **Suspected Underdark entrance (U10):** Deep vaults below the Archive — unknown depth. Thessara decoded 6 sealed site locations. Sub-level 2 was deepest explored.

- Population: ~8,000 (mostly scholars and Archive staff)
- From Homestead: 3.5 days by charter carriage, or 2 hrs to Tidemeet + 3–4 days by ship

### Salthollow
Northeastern coastal university town. Home of the Athenaeum (Lorekeeper Idris Vane's private archive). Smaller than Duskport; no walls, no garrison, centered around the university.

- Population: ~5,000
- From Homestead: 4 days overland (via Harrowgate → Scholars' Coastal Road), or via Tidemeet by ship (~3 days)

### Harrowgate
Inland trade city at the crossroads of the King's Road and the Mirren River. Seat of the Coinwright's Guild (Guildmaster Orvyn Tetch). Largest market in the region.

- Population: ~15,000
- From Homestead: 2 days overland (south through marshes to King's Road)

### Greymoor
Northern mining city in the Greypeak Mountains. Seat of the Pallid Court. Known for iron, silver, and deep-shaft mining. Cold, industrial, isolated. 🕳 **Confirmed Underdark entrance (U2):** Shaft 9 — sealed door at 600 ft depth (found by Donal Crewe). Miners report "singing from the ground" (Durnock).

- Population: ~7,000
- From Homestead: 5 days by charter carriage (Iron Road), or 2 days south to Fenhollow + 3 days north via Thornveil Forest

### Fenhollow
Northwestern marshland settlement and base of the Thornwatch (Captain Jorik Brenn, reinstated). Not a true city, but raised villages linked by boardwalks and causeways through the marshes.

- Population: ~3,000 (distributed)
- From Homestead: 20 minutes walk / immediate vicinity

### Port Veldmere
Southern port city across the Hearthcoast Sea from Duskport. Seat of Marchion Drael Sulwick's former trade empire (dismantled). Controls southern sea routes.

- Population: ~18,000
- From Homestead: 1.5 days via Tidemeet river route, or 3 days overland to Duskport + 3 days by ship

### Ashport
Southern fishing and timber port. Warm climate. Independent — no civic alliance. Friendly to traders. Tropical hardwood, citrus, spices, fish oil.

- Population: ~6,000
- From Homestead: 4 days sail south of Veldmere (~7 days total from Tidemeet)

### Coral Haven
Island port on a reef in the southern waters. Pearl divers. Isolated, suspicious of outsiders. Revka has one contact.

- Population: ~3,000
- From Homestead: 7 days sail south of Veldmere (~10 days total from Tidemeet)

### Sunmere
Major southern city. Warm, wealthy. Primary surface-Underdark trade nexus — Drow merchants (including Vel'sharen agents) surface here. Silk, dyes, exotic spices, adamantine, gems. Nhilara's former base of operations.

- Population: ~10,000+
- From Homestead: 10 days sail south of Veldmere (~13 days total from Tidemeet)

## SEAS & WATERWAYS

### The Hearthcoast Sea
The central body of water connecting Duskport, Veldmere, Salthollow, and Aelindra. Busy shipping lanes; Sulwick's fleet impounded, Silver Chain now patrols southern routes.

### The Mirren River
Flows from Greypeak foothills southeast through Harrowgate and toward the coast east of Duskport. Primary inland trade artery; Tetch's guild frozen, under civic oversight.

### The Fenhollow Marshes
Vast wetlands northwest of Harrowgate. Tidal and treacherous; navigable mainly by locals and Thornwatch guides. Three sealed pre-Sundering ruins appear at low tide.

## FORESTS & WILDERNESS

### Thornveil Forest
Dense old-growth forest between Greymoor and Fenhollow. The Iron Road skirts its eastern edge. Interior is largely unmapped; locals avoid it. Orindel hunts along the southern edge near the homestead. 🕳 **Suspected Underdark entrance (U5):** Two sinkholes scouted by Orindel + Seren (Day 170–171). Large sinkhole (~250 ft deep, limestone collapse). Small sinkhole (warm air rising — geothermal or large enclosed space). Both marked on Torven's map.

### The Greypeak Mountains
Northern mountain range. Greymoor sits in a southern valley. Greypeak Pass is the only reliable crossing to lands beyond. The Greypeak Reavers (10–15 bandits) operate in the pass — opportunists, avoid large armed groups. Griffons nest in the high crags (territorial during Highsun breeding season). 🕳 **Suspected Underdark entrance (U4):** Limestone karst geology — riddled with natural caverns. Vaelith: "The mountains are hollow. They always were."

### The Northern Highlands
Barren scrubland and exposed rock beyond Greypeak Pass. No settlements. No trees. Constant wind. The Vigil Stone Road runs north along the ridge. Temperature drops sharply. Animal life thins as you move north — the ward boundary's influence. Ogres reported in the highland scrubland (2–3, territorial). Possible troll in a ravine (unconfirmed). At night, shapes are visible on the eastern ridgeline — entities on the other side of the thinning ward boundary. **Post-Node 12 repair:** entities withdrew from Greyveil Pass ridgelines. Ward dome visible and stable at 72%.

### The Dead Zones (Beyond the Outer Wards)
Lands beyond the outer ward perimeter. Pre-Sundering texts describe them as "lands where death is ambient" — necrotic energy saturates the environment. Prolonged exposure causes exhaustion, necrotic damage, then death. The wards don't just keep things out — they keep the death energy from spreading inward. **Post-repair status:** With all 8 known outer nodes restored and the cascade ended, the ward boundary has stabilized. Dead zones are actively recovering — the necrotic field at Sethrak's Threshold evaporated upon reconstruction, and the Stormcape Dead Zone is clearing after Node 5 reconstruction. Enchanted Dawnwatch rings provide ward-network recognition but not full environmental protection beyond the ward line.

## ROADS & ROUTES

### King's Road
Main overland artery from Duskport north through Harrowgate and onward toward Greymoor.

### Iron Road
Branches from King's Road at Harrowgate and climbs into the Greypeaks toward Greymoor. Rough, steep, wagon-accessible, and slow; ore caravans use it constantly.

### Thornfield Road
Runs north from Fenhollow through Barrowfen and into the northern countryside. Unpaved but well-traveled. Thornfield Dispatch coaching inn located along this road. Dead drop sites and the route to Keth-Varos branch off from here.

### Archivists' Road
Coastal route connecting Aelindra to Salthollow. 2 days overland. Well-maintained by Archive funding.

### Scholars' Coastal Road
Connects Salthollow south to Duskport along the northeastern coast. 3 days overland. Less maintained. Fishing villages along route.

### Vigil Stone Road (Northern Highlands)
Ancient pre-Sundering road running north from Greypeak Pass along the highland ridge toward Greyveil Pass and beyond. Fitted stone, no mortar — Vigil of the Closed Eye construction. Still intact after 1,200+ years. No settlements. Vigil wardens (undead sentinels) still stand guard at intervals. The road is a ward conduit — residual energy keeps the stones warm and the wardens animated. Discovered by the Dawnwatch during the Greyveil expedition. **Extends north past Greyveil Pass to the Warden's Forge (2 days) and branches toward Sethrak's Threshold (Node 11).**

### Tidemeet ⚓
River mouth where the Fenhollow river meets the Hearthcoast Sea. Sandy beach on the south bank, rocky tide pools on the north. Half-day walk south of Fenhollow (2 hours by cart). Coastal vessels dock here — sea route to Aelindra (3–4 days).

### Thornfield Dispatch 🏨
Coaching inn and courier service on the Thornfield Road, north of Fenhollow. Operated by Harlen Grubb. Used as the logistics channel for the Watcher's necromantic operation — dead drops, payments, and correspondence. Grubb cooperated with the Dawnwatch. The courier route is now compromised.

Ruined temple of the Order of the Ashen Veil. Located in Ashenmere Valley, hills east of Greymoor (3 days' walk). Destroyed by Veradis Kael's necromantic attack — 17 members of the Order killed. Small hamlet on the Thornfield Road, half-day north of Fenhollow. Site of Veradis Kael's catalyst field tests — 5 graves disturbed, 3 zombies raised and subsequently destroyed by the Dawnwatch. Village cemetery now quiet.

## OUTER WARD NETWORK
