# Full Planet Map — The Known World and Beyond

> **Scale:** 1 hex = 120 miles (~5 days sail, ~5 hexes on the continental scaled-map)
> **Grid:** 30 columns (A–AD) × 20 rows (1–20). Origin A1 = far northwest.
> **Source:** The World Intelligence (Day 184, 1253 AS). First complete planetary map in history.
> **Continental detail:** See `scaled-map.md` (1 hex = 24 mi) for the Known Continent.

## Deep Ocean — The Unknowns

The World's statement: **"There are things in my deep water that I do not fully understand. They are old. They do not walk on my surface. They swim in my blood."**

| What We Know | Source |
|-------------|--------|
| Deep-ocean entities exist | The World |
| They are old — possibly as old as the World itself | The World |
| They are not surface-dwellers — entirely aquatic | The World |
| The World doesn't fully understand them | The World (unusual — it understands everything else on the planet) |
| Voidmaws (resonance parasites) may be related | Dawnwatch encounter at Seedhome |
| 78 deep-ocean planting sites exist | Gardener's 412-site chart |

**DM note:** The deep ocean is the last true unknown. The World knows its own stone, its own geometry, its own surface. But the deep water holds things that predate even the World's awareness — or that exist in a way the World's geometric consciousness cannot fully parse. Future arc material.
