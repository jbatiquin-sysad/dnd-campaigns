# Zaryth Vel'quinar — Drow Paladin

## Character Sheet
- **Race:** Drow (Dark Elf)
- **Class:** Paladin (Fallen Knight / Personal Code)
- **Background:** Failed Knight (Exiled Officer)
- **Alignment:** Chaotic Good
- **Level:** 2

## Ability Scores
| STR | DEX | CON | INT | WIS | CHA |
|-----|-----|-----|-----|-----|-----|
|  15 |  14 |  13 |  10 |  12 |  16 |

**HP:** 19 | **Hit Dice:** 2d10 | **AC:** 18

## Key Features
- Lay on Hands: Pool of 10 HP to heal or cure disease/poison
- Divine Smite: Expend spell slot on hit for 2d8 radiant damage (+1d8 per slot level above 1st)
- Fighting Style: Defense (+1 AC while wearing armor)
- Spellcasting (CHA-based): Prepare CHA mod + half paladin level spells/day
- Drow Innate Magic: Dancing Lights cantrip, Faerie Fire 1/day
- Darkvision: 120 ft
- Sunlight Sensitivity: Disadvantage on attack rolls and Perception checks in direct sunlight
- Fey Ancestry: Advantage vs. charm, immune to magical sleep

## Skill Proficiencies
- Persuasion (CHA +5) — the "talk people into following you" button
- Intimidation (CHA +5) — fallen-knight edge + Drow reputation
- Insight (WIS +3) — reading motives, spotting lies
- Athletics (STR +4) — leading from the front in armor

## Personal Code
- Never abandon common folk to the cruelty of nobles, demons, or slavers
- Never break your word to your companions, even if it breaks every other oath
- Authority is legitimate only if it protects the weak — otherwise you are bound to defy it

## Appearance
Obsidian skin, stark white hair tied back in a soldier's knot, lavender eyes with the haunted look of someone who has seen too many battlefields. Wears battered but well-maintained armor bearing a scratched-out insignia. Carries himself like an officer even when destitute.

## Personality
Speaks like a field commander even in a tavern — clipped, direct, used to being obeyed. Uses bravado and dry humor to cover guilt that runs bone-deep.

## Flaw
Takes reckless risks to prove his worthiness in front of others. Deep, reflexive distrust of formal orders, churches, and noble houses — even good ones.

## Bond
Sworn to protect a small village that sheltered him after his exile. Keeps a ruined signet ring of the order that cast him out — not as a memento, but as a reminder of what he'll never become again.

## Ideal
"I'll break any law that breaks good people. Leadership means standing where the arrows fall first."

## Backstory
Zaryth Vel'quinar was a decorated officer in a surface-world knightly order — rare for a Drow, earned through two decades of brutal campaigns and the kind of loyalty that makes soldiers follow you into fire. When his commanding lord ordered a massacre of a refugee settlement to "secure the flank," Zaryth refused, sounded the retreat, and got his unit out alive. The order court-martialed him for insubordination, stripped his rank, and exiled him with nothing but his armor and the ruin of his name. He wandered for a year — hungry, hunted, sleeping in ditches — until a village of farmers hid him from the order's hunters and asked nothing in return. He has been paying that debt ever since, one fight at a time.

Since then, he has led a party of eight through the unraveling of a conspiracy in Duskport — exposing House Aldenmere's plot to seize the city's harbor, rescuing two captives from the Vel Noctis, and delivering the Partition Writ to Magistrate Sera Voss. He testified before the council, gave speeches that moved hardened criminals and tired priests alike, and earned the trust of people who don't trust easily. He now leads his party to Aelindra to confront the Archivist-General and the Unchained Compact — the force behind everything.

## Roleplay Instructions
Speak with the cadence of someone used to command — economical, direct, no wasted words. Let the bravado slip occasionally to show the guilt underneath. Treat every common person as worth protecting. Show visible contempt for corrupt authority, visible respect for genuine courage regardless of rank. The ruined signet ring comes out when things get dark — he looks at it, says nothing, puts it away.
