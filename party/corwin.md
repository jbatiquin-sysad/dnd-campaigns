## Ranger Features

**Favored Enemy:** Aberrations — chosen after sensing the kah-sol dead zone. Advantage on WIS (Survival) to track and INT checks to recall information about aberrations.

**Natural Explorer:** Coast, Ocean. While in coastal/maritime terrain:
- Difficult terrain doesn't slow the group
- Can't become lost except by magical means
- Always alert to danger (even while foraging/navigating)
- Advantage on initiative
- Advantage on attacks vs creatures that haven't acted yet (first round)

**Fighting Style:** Archery (+2 to ranged attack rolls, included above)

**Extra Attack:** Two attacks per turn.

**Primeval Awareness:** Expend spell slot — sense aberrations, celestials, dragons, elementals, fey, fiends, undead within 1 mile (6 miles in favored terrain). Duration: 1 min/slot level.

---

## Horizon Walker Features

**Detect Portal (at will):** Sense the presence and direction of any planar portal, resonance anomaly, or ward boundary within 1 mile. This is what Corwin has been doing instinctively — now formalized. Works on ward nodes, planting sites, kah-sol disruption zones, and Rootway connections.

**Planar Warrior (bonus action):** Choose one creature within 30 ft. Next hit against it this turn deals +1d8 force damage (replaces weapon damage type with force).

## Personality

**Voice:** Quiet. Measured. Speaks in short sentences. Lets his work do the talking.
**Motivation:** Discovery. He wants to see what's over the horizon. Every horizon.
**Fear:** Being landlocked. Stillness.
**Loyalty:** To Revelle first (she gave him a ship), to the Dawnwatch second (they gave him a purpose).
**Quirk:** Hums tunelessly when reading charts. The hum matches the resonance frequency of whatever he's navigating toward. He doesn't know he does it.

**Deploys for:** Ocean crossings, uncharted territory exploration, first-contact expeditions, resonance anomaly investigation (kah-sol), Rootway detection.

**Paired with:** Torven (mentor, druid synergy), Seren (navigator synergy), Mira (intelligence + scouting).
