## Combat Tactics

- **Opening:** Faerie Fire on clustered enemies (advantage for entire party), then Rage
- **Rage + Claws:** 3 attacks per round (Extra Attack + bonus claw). With Rage: 1d6+4 each = 3d6+12 per round.
- **Rage + Bite (tanking):** 2 attacks. Below half HP: regain 3 HP per bite hit. Halved physical damage from Rage.
- **Rage + Tail (defense):** 2 attacks at 10 ft reach. Reaction: +1d8 AC vs one attack.
- **Sneak Attack:** 2d6 on one scimitar/crossbow hit per turn (finesse, need advantage or adjacent ally). Stacks with Rage bonus damage on melee.
- **Skirmisher:** Reaction move half speed (20 ft) when enemy ends adjacent. Never pinned down.
- **Cunning Action:** Bonus Dash (80 ft round), Disengage, or Hide.
- **Darkness:** Cast before rage. Invisible tanking (Drow darkvision sees through own Darkness? No — Darkness blocks darkvision. Use for enemy disruption, not self-benefit).
- **Faerie Fire + party:** Advantage for Mira/Thessaly Sneak Attack. Advantage for all melee.
- **Grapple (Beast bite):** Rage advantage on STR checks. Bite + grapple to hold targets for party focus fire.

## Personality

**Voice:** Low, precise, economical. Doesn't waste words. Dry humor that surfaces at the worst possible moment. Twelve years undercover taught her to read a room in three seconds and lie without blinking. She's trying to unlearn the lying part.

**Traits:**
- Watches exits. Always. Sits with her back to the wall.

## Backstory

Nhilara Vel'quinar was born into House Vel'quinar in Vel'arath — a minor house that followed Eilistraee in secret. When the house fell, Nhilara was already on the surface, embedded in Vel'sharen's outer trade circle as a liaison. She was 19.

For twelve years she maintained her cover, moving between surface ports — Tidemeet, Veldmere, Salthollow — facilitating Vel'sharen trade interests while quietly feeding information to Vel'quinar survivors through dead drops only she and Ilyana knew about. She believed the house was destroyed. She kept the drops active anyway.

When the Dawnwatch repaired the ward nodes and the network began singing, Nhilara felt it through the old Vel'quinar resonance markers her mother taught her to read. She followed the signal to Tidemeet, where Dawnwatch Trading Company had just opened a warehouse.

She walked in and asked for Zaryth by name. Day 151, 1249 AS. She came home.

## Defining Moment
At Blacktide Inlet, Nhilara erupted from the sand in beast-form — claws extended, rage burning — ten feet from a 3,000-year-old Binder. She was the only one still standing after Ashara's Ward Pulse flattened three party members. She held the line alone. Her final claw strike ended the fight. Non-lethal. "Argument's over."

## Downtime
Watches Ashara. But differently now. Brings meals. Sits outside the containment room. On the eighth day, brought Ashara a piece of glass Velyndra made. Maintains the night watch rotation for the Vel'quinar sentries. Sharpens her scimitar every evening — the dark steel holds an edge like nothing she's ever carried.
