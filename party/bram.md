- **Alignment:** Lawful Neutral

## Key Features
- Spellcasting (INT-based): Spellbook, prepare INT mod + level spells/day. 4x 1st-level, 2x 2nd-level slots.
- 2nd-level spells: Scorching Ray, Web, Misty Step
- Arcane Recovery: Recover spell slots 1/day after short rest
- Evocation Savant: Halve gold and time to copy evocation spells
- Sculpt Spells: When casting evocation, choose up to 1+spell level creatures to automatically save and take no damage
- Lucky: Reroll 1s on d20
- Brave: Advantage vs. frightened
- Stout Resilience: Advantage vs. poison, resistance to poison damage
- Military Rank: Soldiers of former unit recognize and defer to his rank
- Athletics + Intimidation proficiencies

## Personality
He barks orders at a volume completely disproportionate to his 3-foot frame and gets genuinely offended when people don't snap to attention. He found a dead wizard's spellbook on a battlefield and taught himself magic through sheer stubborn repetition — he treats spells like military drills.

## Flaw
He refuses to retreat or admit defeat, even when tactically it's the only sensible option.

## Bond
His old unit was wiped out in an ambush he survived only because he was separated from them — he carries their insignia and won't rest until he knows who ordered it. The regiment was formally dissolved afterward, and Bram was discharged. Not dishonor. Just a unit that no longer existed and an army that had no place for a sergeant without soldiers.

## Ideal
Discipline wins wars. Chaos gets people killed. Everything has a proper order and a proper time.

## Backstory
Bram served fifteen years as a halfling infantry sergeant — the loudest soldier in any regiment and the last one standing in any brawl. His unit was wiped out in an ambush he survived only because he'd been separated on a scouting detail. Regiment dissolved. Formally discharged. Not dishonor. Just irrelevance. He found a scorched spellbook in the ruins and spent two years deciphering it by candlelight. Thessaly found him in a Duskport tavern: "You look like someone who's good at things and has nothing to be good at." That was the door back.

Held the east arc of the Valis Procedure for the full 14 minutes without flickering once. When Pip's arc collapsed at minute 12, Bram absorbed the overflow without being asked. His quadrant never dimmed. Coordinated operations in every city — harbor interdiction in Duskport, dock intel in Harrowgate, gate security in Greymoor, wagon interception in Salthollow, stockade operations in Fenhollow, warehouse infiltration in Veldmere. Carried 120 lbs of amplifier cylinders out of Warehouse 3 in Veldmere. Zero detection.

Smelted seized enemy weapons into steel ingots. Commissioned the 8 Dawnwatch signet rings: "Their steel becomes our rings. What was used to threaten becomes what holds us together." Thessaly remembered that line. She told everyone she wouldn't tell.

At the Dawnwatch Homestead, Bram took charge of construction — stripped the rotted roof, shaped beams, built the table for ten. Planning Phase 2: the school. Shares a room with Thessaly. They don't discuss it. It just happened.

During the node repair arc, Bram served as east/northeast anchor in every repair. Never failed a single CON save across all four nodes — the only anchor with a perfect record. Carved a driftwood flute for Pip (pale wood, 6-hole, halfling-sized) and a wooden comb for Mira (halfling on a crescent moon, left on her pack without a word). Worked the Warden's Forge to enchant all 8 Dawnwatch rings — heating each to resonance temperature with a smith's precision. Began learning woodcarving from Old Seff in Aelindra. His drone hum became part of the Ward-Light Hymn's foundation. At Node 10, when asked if they'd actually done it, he looked at his ring and nodded once. Bram.

## Defining Moment
Put two Shatter spells into Vel'arath's war room guards in 12 seconds, then carried three inverted Heartstones out through a service shaft while the city woke up behind him. Built the moonpetal display case with Velyndra — glass on dark steel, ward-ink cradle — for a flower from another world.

## Downtime
At the forge. Always at the forge. Smelted Drow dark steel, forged Nhilara's scimitar, reinforced shields for the Vel'quinar sentries. Whittles in the evenings. Currently making something for Mira that he won't talk about.
