# Philippine-Themed Monsters for D&D 5e

This guide provides descriptions and 5e reskinning suggestions for iconic creatures from Philippine mythology.

---

## 1. Aswang (The Shape-shifting Ghoul)
The Aswang is a versatile predator that appears human by day but transforms into a monster (dog, pig, or winged beast) at night. They are known for their long, proboscis-like tongues used to drain organs.

*   **5e Reskin:** **Vampire Spawn** or **Ghoul**.
*   **Modifications:**
    *   **Shapechanger:** Can polymorph into a Large dog or pig (use **Dire Wolf** or **Giant Boar** stats).
    *   **Proboscis Drain:** Replace the bite with a 10ft reach "Tongue Drain" (Necrotic damage).
    *   **Weaknesses:** Disadvantage on attacks if garlic or salt is present within 10ft.

## 2. Manananggal (The Self-Segmenter)
A terrifying female spirit that detaches her upper torso from her lower body at night. She grows bat wings and flies in search of victims, leaving her vulnerable lower half hidden.

*   **5e Reskin:** **Harpy** or **Vampire**.
*   **Modifications:**
    *   **Detach (Trait):** As an action, she splits. The upper body gains a fly speed of 40ft. The lower body is immobile and incapacitated.
    *   **Salt Vulnerability:** If salt, ash, or garlic is sprinkled on her severed lower torso, she cannot reattach and takes 2d10 radiant damage at the start of each turn until she dies or the salt is removed.
    *   **Target:** Primarily hunts sleeping or pregnant targets.

## 3. Tikbalang (The Horse-Headed Trickster)
A tall, gangly humanoid with the head and hooves of a horse. They dwell in deep forests and use illusions to lead travelers astray.

*   **5e Reskin:** **Centaur** or **Nightmare**.
*   **Modifications:**
    *   **Innate Spellcasting:** *Minor Illusion* (at will), *Phantasmal Force* (3/day), *Hallucinatory Terrain* (1/day).
    *   **Labyrinthine Trick:** Travelers in the Tikbalang's forest must pass a DC 14 Wisdom save or become "turned around," traveling in circles unless they wear their clothes inside out.
    *   **Taming:** A player who succeeds in a series of Athletics checks to "ride" the Tikbalang can claim one of its golden hairs to command it.

## 4. Kapre (The Tree Giant)
A 7-9ft tall giant with dark, hairy skin who sits in large trees (like Balete or Acacia) smoking a cigar. They are often neutral but can be mischievous or protective.

*   **5e Reskin:** **Stone Giant** or **Hill Giant**.
*   **Modifications:**
    *   **Invisibility:** Can cast *Invisibility* on itself at will while touching a tree.
    *   **Cigar Smoke:** As a bonus action, creates a 20ft radius of *Fog Cloud* centered on itself.
    *   **Nature's Ward:** Gains +2 AC and Advantage on saves while within 30ft of its home tree.

## 5. Nuno sa Punso (The Mound Dweller)
Small, elderly humanoid spirits that live in anthills or termite mounds. They are easily offended by those who step on their homes without saying "Tabi-tabi po" (Excuse me).

*   **5e Reskin:** **Goblin** or **Gnome (Deep)**.
*   **Modifications:**
    *   **Curse (Reaction):** When its mound is disturbed, the Nuno can cast *Bestow Curse* (DC 13) or *Blindness/Deafness* on the offender.
    *   **Earth Meld:** Can move through nonmagical, unworked earth and stone as if it were air.

## 6. Tiyanak (The Demon Child)
A vampiric creature that takes the form of a helpless infant. Once a traveler picks it up, it transforms, revealing sharp teeth and claws to attack.

*   **5e Reskin:** **Quasit** or **Shadow**.
*   **Modifications:**
    *   **False Appearance:** While it remains motionless in infant form, it is indistinguishable from a human baby.
    *   **Mimicry:** Can mimic the sound of a crying baby perfectly.
    *   **Latching Bite:** When it hits with a bite, it becomes attached. While attached, it deals automatic damage each turn.

## 7. Bakunawa (The Moon-Eater)
A colossal sea serpent of legend that rises from the ocean to swallow the moon. It represents eclipses and maritime disasters.

*   **5e Reskin:** **Dragon Turtle** or **Kraken** (depending on level).
*   **Modifications:**
    *   **Swallow Moon (Legendary Action):** Can create magical darkness in a 100ft radius.
    *   **Tidal Wave:** Can cast *Control Water* at will.
    *   **Amphibious:** Can breathe air and water.

## 8. Sigbin (The Shadow Hound)
Goat-like creatures with large ears and long tails that walk backwards. They are often kept as "familiars" by powerful witches or Aswangs.

*   **5e Reskin:** **Hell Hound** or **Worg**.
*   **Modifications:**
    *   **Shadow Blend:** In dim light or darkness, the Sigbin can Hide as a bonus action.
    *   **Blood Sense:** Has advantage on Perception checks to track any creature that has less than its maximum hit points.
