# Zaryth's Speech — The Naming of Vel'tarn

> **Occasion:** Ember Night, 15th Ashfall (Day 195), 1253 AS
> **Location:** North Meadow, Vel'tarn (formerly Dawnwatch Homestead), Fenhollow
> **Audience:** ~100 — Dawnwatch, Vel'quinar household, Fenhollow villagers, Southern Shelf, Stormcape, Vel'sharen
> **DM:** Aldric Voss, The Chronicler (Session 11)

---

"Thank you for coming. All of you. From the marshes, from the Shelf, from the cliffs, from the deep roads, from across the sea. Some of you walked three days to be here. Some of you walked three thousand years."

"Six years ago, ten strangers sat around a table in Duskport and decided to do something about a world that was breaking. We didn't know each other. We didn't know what we were doing. We just knew that someone had to start."

"Tonight, we are naming this place."

"We are calling it **Vel'tarn**."

"In the old tongue of my people — the language I was born to, the language I was exiled for refusing to use as a weapon — Vel'tarn means: *those who choose peace.*"

"Not those who were given peace. Not those who found it by accident. Those who *chose* it. When war was easier. When the blade was already in hand. When every instinct, every tradition, every voice in the dark said *fight, take, dominate.*"

*[Draws Dawnsedge from the earth. Holds it up. Lightning and radiance flicker along the blade.]*

"I carry a sword. I have carried one since I was old enough to hold it. I have used it. I will use it again."

*[Lowers the blade. Rests the flat across both palms.]*

"But this place is not named for the sword. It is named for the moment you put it down."

"In Fenhollow's marsh and mist — in the fog and the mud and the cold — we learned something. Every life here is called not just to survive the blade, but to lay it down. To stand watch so others can sleep. To become a warden of peace for those who cannot defend themselves."

"Some of us came from places where peace was a death sentence. Where choosing not to fight meant choosing to die. We are here because we refused to accept that. And we are still here — all of us, every one — because this marsh, this table, these people proved us right."

"Vel'tarn is not a Drow word because I am Drow. It is a Drow word because the people who needed it most are the ones who never had it. Tonight, they do."

*[Kneels. Places the World's Hearthstone at the base of the pyre.]*

"This stone was given to us by the World itself. Four billion years old. It asked us one thing."

"It said: *'I would very much like not to be alone anymore.'*"

"So tonight — Ember Night, the turning of the year toward dark — we answer. With fire. With music. With a name carved in stone above our gate. And with this:"

*[Stands. Raises Dawnsedge.]*

**"Vel'tarn stands. Not because we are strong. Because we chose."**

*[Strikes the pyre. The ward-threaded bonfire ignites — blue-white and gold, spiraling fifteen feet into the autumn sky.]*
