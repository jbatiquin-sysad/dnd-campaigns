## Summary

### Homestead Operations (Days 46–48)

Dawnwatch established daily operations at the homestead. Zaryth began alchemy training (10 days). Mira began herbalism on-the-job training. Aldric began mining training. Bram began smithing. Pip began inscription. Thessaly began tailoring. All party members assigned side hustles for income.

Aldric shared his full account of the Temple of Quiet Passage attack: the Order of the Ashen Veil (healers), Ashenmere Valley east of Greymoor, a visitor 10 days before the attack, the figure on the ridge with a staff, 17 dead. The visitor's ledger was destroyed.

### Barrowfen Investigation (Days 49–50)

Thessaly reported a rumor: "restless graves" north of Fenhollow near Barrowfen. Expedition party: Zaryth, Mira, Sylvara, Orindel.

**Barrowfen village:** 5 graves disturbed from the inside. Bodies walking east into the tree line. A hooded woman with a dark staff passed through ~12 days prior. Mira scouted the cemetery — tracks leading east, dark catalyst residue on disturbed soil.

**Sylvara analyzed the residue (Arcana 23):** Arcane necromancy. Delayed-activation catalyst — bone ash, grave salts, rendered fat. Pre-applied, triggered remotely. Formal training required.

**The hollow:** 3 zombies found standing motionless in a birch wood. Compulsion fading. Party engaged — surprise round. All 3 destroyed in 2 rounds. Zero damage taken. Radiant damage from Zaryth (Divine Smite) and Orindel (Sacred Flame) bypassed Undead Fortitude.

**The campsite:** Necromancer's base camp found in a rock overhang. Evidence recovered: glass vials with moth-over-crescent maker's mark, grinding stone, rendered fat, charcoal map showing route to a barrow, torn journal page referencing Ashenmere and "the barrow at Keth-Varos."

**Ambush (pre-dawn, Day 50):** Party set overnight ambush at the campsite. The necromancer returned. Surprise round — 49 damage in one round. Subdued non-lethally. Bound and interrogated.

### Veradis Kael — Interrogation (Day 50)

**Identity:** Veradis Kael, former scholar-initiate of the Order of the Ashen Veil. Expelled 6 years before the temple attack for researching necrotic boundary mechanics.

**Confession:** Solely responsible for the Ashenmere attack. Designed the catalyst for solo remote operation. Entered via eastern drainage channel. Claims mass casualties were unintended — activation overflow.

**Keth-Varos:** Pre-settlement ossuary barrow, ~300 remains. Barrowfen was calibration. Keth-Varos was the proof-of-concept demonstration.

**The buyer ("Thornfield commission"):** Anonymous. Dead drop on Thornfield Road. Payment via Veldmere exchange under name "Maren Holt." Buyer knew about Ashenmere, the Sealed Quorum's collapse, and the Dawnwatch.

**Moth-and-crescent:** Veradis's personal brand, not an organization.

### Aldric Confronts Veradis (Day 51)

Aldric visited Veradis in the root cellar. Identified himself as the survivor from the herbarium. Named the dead: Brother Velan, Sister Dara, Prior Aldenmuth. Told her she didn't prove a principle — she murdered seventeen healers. Did not kill her. Walked out.

The Dawnwatch deliberated. Zaryth's decision: turn her over to Greymoor magistrate with proper wards. Fenhollow militia escort arranged. Sylvara prepared warded manacles (Arcane Lock + divine seal).
