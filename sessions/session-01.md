# Session 1 — The Partition Writ

**Date:** 2026-03-28
**DM:** Aldric Voss, The Chronicler
**Duration:** 27 turns

## Summary

What began as a 200gp debt collection at midnight became a 10-year conspiracy to seize Duskport's harbor district.

The party arrived at the Tallow & Timber Inn to find a Crimson Ledger collector named Cassin Vael waiting for Pip Tanglethorn's overdue debt. Through investigation, intimidation, and a nat 20 Insight from Sylvara, the party uncovered that the Vel Noctis — a pre-Sundering mercantile guild believed dissolved — were the real force behind the account. They weren't collecting money. They were collecting leverage.

The trail led to a Hollow Court warehouse on the south docks, a pre-Sundering Undercroft beneath the city, and a sealed Repository containing the Partition Writ of Duskport — a civic founding document establishing that the harbor district can never be privately owned. House Aldenmere had been positioning to seize the harbor for three years. The Writ was the only thing stopping them.

The party fought through Vel Noctis operatives, rescued Maret Soll (a scribe held captive for two months as a translator) and Duvash (a dockworker held for five months as a tunnel guide), and secured the Writ at dawn.

Cassin Vael closed the account and walked away. Vael Orun — the night-priest who had been orchestrating events for ten years — revealed that Lord Edric Aldenmere, the enemy house's second son, had been her secret informant all along.
