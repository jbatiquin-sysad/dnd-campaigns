# Week 1 — The Partition Writ & The Civic Trust

**In-Game:** Night 1 (midnight) through Day 1 (morning)
**Sessions:** S1 (27 turns), S2 (10 turns)
**DMs:** Aldric Voss, The Chronicler (S1) / Mara Solenne, The Storyteller (S2)
**Party Level:** 1

## Summary

A 200gp debt collection at midnight unraveled a 10-year conspiracy to seize Duskport's harbor district.

Cassin Vael, a Crimson Ledger collector, came for Pip Tanglethorn's overdue debt at the Tallow & Timber Inn. Investigation revealed the Vel Noctis — a pre-Sundering guild believed dissolved — were using the debt as leverage. The trail led to a Hollow Court warehouse, a pre-Sundering Undercroft, and a sealed Repository containing the Partition Writ — a civic founding document protecting the harbor from private ownership.

The party fought through Vel Noctis operatives, rescued Maret Soll (2 months captive) and Duvash (5 months captive), and secured the Writ at dawn. Cassin closed the account. Vael Orun — the night-priest who orchestrated events for 10 years — revealed Lord Edric Aldenmere as her secret informant.

At dawn, the party split to deliver the Writ to Magistrate Voss and extract Edric before his family reached him. Harwick, the family solicitor, was revealed as Vael Orun's 10-year partner. He'd burned the Vel Noctis contracts but kept a deed of civic trust proving the family always knew the Writ was legitimate. Edric testified against his own family (nat 20). Voss froze House Aldenmere's harbor acquisitions and opened a civic inquiry.
