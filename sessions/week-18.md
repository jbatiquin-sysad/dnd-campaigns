# Week 18 — The Wildcard's Arc: Scouts, Copper Hair, Ashport, Ashara

**In-Game:** Days 181–204, 1249 AS (1st–24th Ashfall)
**Session:** S8 (Corvus Chance, The Wildcard)
**Party Level:** 3 (Nhilara: 8)

## Summary

Twenty-three days. The Dawnwatch dismantled a three-thousand-year conspiracy, captured a Binder older than history, and came home with ten Heartstones and a song.

### Scout Intercept (Days 181–182)
Nhilara intercepted Vel'sharen scout dead drop. Two scouts (Velyndra, Taelar) tracked to marsh narrows, ambushed, captured in 6 seconds. Target package revealed Phase Two: 6-agent insertion via G6 sinkhole, objective Ashara station + Zaryth's signet ring. Handler: Matron Zhael Vel'sharen.

### Operation Copper Hair (Day 182)
Rescue of Sylla Vel'nessra (age 7) from Vel'arath crèche. Team: Zaryth, Mira, Nhilara. Route: Deep Road → Crucible → branch tunnel → Station Four (discovered, 44% dormant, beneath Vel'arath old quarter). Crèche extraction: 8 minutes, zero detection. Station Four = Vel'sharen blind spot (no northern patrols). Velyndra + Taelar defected. Sylla enrolled in school (#15).

### Vel'sharen Network Sweep (Day 182)
Two simultaneous operations. Veldmere: Drael + Syvra (spice merchant cover) + courier captured at Anchor's Rest. Zero combat. Greymoor: 2 agents fled, killed Corporal Wren Hale (garrison scout, age 23). Pursued, captured at Thornveil forest edge. Bram Sleep + Mira Shadow Bolt. Full surface network dismantled: 7 prisoners total.

### Seven-Layer Countermeasures (Days 184–187)
G6 sealed (ward-locked, alarmed). Ashara station fortified (triple-locked, remote hard-seal). Station Four covert (standing order). Blacktide Inlet surveillance (Revka/Silver Chain). Ashenmere verification (Cole). Veldmere three interrogated (Harlen Cray identified, ships identified, Phase Three timeline: before first frost). Full network alerted (9 cities).

### Ashport Operation (Days 188–196)
Sailed south on *Eilistraee's Grace*. Safehouse established (Bessa's cottage). 3-day recon: Cray's chandlery, Thornback Gulch "mine" (12 guards, chemical activity, Binding frequency). Sea cave discovered (Seren). Workshop raided: 3 inverted Heartstone blanks, 1 real Heartstone (Ashenmere 4th), V.K.'s journal (inversion formula), tunnel map, alchemical equipment destroyed. Alarm tripped (faerzress powder). Workshop sealed.

### Anchor Point (Day 196)
Descended to pre-Binder stone circle (400 ft depth). 6-slot basin — prototype of the ward network. Vaelen's projection appeared: creator of the ward network, trapped by her student Ashara for 3,000 years. Vaelen gave 7th Heartstone (her original). Anchor Point sealed (3-layer ward-lock). Promise to return.

### Vel'arath Raid — Team Beta (Day 201)
Aldric, Sylvara, Bram, Pip, Torven. Deep Road → Station Four → service shaft → war room. 3 inverted Heartstones recovered from Zhael's warded chest + ancient scroll case (Ashara's, unread). 2 inner circle guards defeated (2 rounds). Exfil before dawn survey team arrived. Vel'arath on alert — Team Beta already gone.

### Battle of Blacktide Inlet (Day 202)
Team Alpha ambush. Fog cover (d100=62). Ashara arrived with Thessara Vael + Veradis Kael. Zaryth confronted Ashara on the pier, talked Veradis and Thessara into defecting. Combat: Mira opened with nat 20 crit (28 damage, never seen). Nhilara erupted from sand in beast-form. Ashara's Ward Pulse (22 force, DC 17) dropped Seren to 2 HP, Thessaly to 2 HP, Zaryth to 6 HP. Ashara Misty Stepped to flee. Binding Lance dropped Zaryth to 0 HP. Mira brought Ashara to 2 HP. Nhilara's final claw strike (non-lethal) ended it. 2 rounds. 18 seconds. Ashara captured alive. Zero Dawnwatch deaths.

### Aftermath (Days 202–204)
Ashara bound, ward-suppressed. Veradis + Thessara surrendered. All confiscated. Thessaly collected 2,000 gp from Maren Holt proxy at Veldmere Exchange. At-sea conversation with Ashara: the Resonant (civilizations beyond the boundary), the moonpetal locket, 3,217 years of existence. Orindel composed The Blacktide Ode (acapella, 4 voices). Celebration on deck with last bottle of Vel'quinar moonwine. Sailing home.

## Key Discoveries

- **Station Four:** 4th keeper station beneath Vel'arath. 44% dormant. Branch tunnel from Crucible (2 mi). Vel'sharen blind spot.
- **Vaelen:** Creator of the ward network. Alive. Bound at Anchor Point for 3,000 years. Projection only. Gave 7th Heartstone.
- **Ashara:** Vaelen's former student. 3,217 years old. Binder. Spent 11 years with the Resonant before the Sundering. Believes boundary is unjust. Trapped Vaelen. Escaped during Sundering. Built entire conspiracy (necromancer funding, Zhael alliance, inverted stones, Anchor Point plan).
- **The Resonant:** Civilizations beyond the ward boundary. Frequency-based. Crystallized-sound cities. Non-hostile (per Ashara). Status unknown after 3,000 years.
- **Anchor Point:** Pre-Binder prototype. 6-slot basin. Older than the ward network. The original that the Binders copied.
- **Inverted Heartstones:** Anti-phase resonance stones. Designed to create destructive interference in the ward network. 6 manufactured (all secured by Dawnwatch).
