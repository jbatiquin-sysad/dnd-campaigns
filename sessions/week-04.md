## Summary

### Calder's Gambit (Duskport, Days 16–17)

The party returned to find Calder had launched a coordinated three-phase strike: burn inquiry evidence at the Temple of the Balanced Scale, break three captives from holding cells, and assassinate Magistrate Voss.

The party split three ways:
- **Protect Voss:** Serevane neutralized rooftop watchers. Sylvara identified the assassin (Arcana 23). Thessaly lured him to a corridor (Deception 25) and dropped him non-lethal. Voss safe.
- **Intercept Ship:** Mira scouted harbor. Bram coordinated watch cordon. Ship seized, 3 captives recaptured, harbor chain raised.
- **Take Calder:** Zaryth and Aldric confronted Calder at the Salted Crown. Calder confessed: "Because my father asked me to." Handed to Voss with due process.

Post-operations: abjuration amulet assigned to Mira. Thessaly's list revealed (7 targets, House Aldenmere was #4). Sealed letter confirmed Duskport was "proof of method." Inside leak (Peytr Hask) identified and recovered. Serevane provided 5 regional handler names. Party received 8x Letters of Credential from Voss.

### The Road (Days 18–20)

Three-day journey via King's Road. Bandits at Thornhook Curve intimidated into standing down (zero combat). 83gp earned in road jobs.

### The Harrowgate Operation (Days 20–22)

Two days of reconnaissance under cover. Renn Corvath identified as senior advisor controlling all fund transfers. Night barge weapons shipments to Greymoor discovered. Sable Wick and Brother Callum recruited. Maren Holt located in Guild-owned tannery.

Operation night — four simultaneous phases:
1. **Ledger Extraction:** Mira + Thessaly + Sylvara. Amulet bypassed ward. Clearinghouse ledger AND handler journal extracted. Zero detection.
2. **Shipment Interdiction:** Zaryth + Aldric + Bram. City watch arrived (d100=81). 11 detained. Cargo seized: weapons, 600gp coin, mining equipment.
3. **Maren Holt Rescue:** Orindel solo. Guard neutralized non-lethal. Maren rescued.
4. **Civic Trigger:** Thessaly presented evidence to Alderman Kellan (Persuasion 24). Guild frozen. Corvath arrested fleeing at north gate.

Zero casualties across all phases. Reeve's reward: 200gp bounty + commendations + provisioning writ + civic barge passage.

### The River North — Greymoor (Days 22–25)

Two days upriver on the civic barge *Millstone*. Party worked cover jobs (105gp) while recruiting allies: Captain Torva Gild (dock logistics), Donal Crewe (Shaft 9 guide), Sister Maren (temple courier), Vella Drace (court clerk with 6 months fraudulent permits). Sylvara identified the Crucible — an Archive containment facility housing a Binding Shard, a Sundering-era continental ward keystone.

**Operation Crucible** — four simultaneous phases at dawn: Shaft 9 ventilation shaft descent (Mira lifted the resonance key off Blackveil's table, Sleight of Hand 23), gate secured (Thessaly + Bram + 6 miners), courthouse filing (Sylvara + Sister Maren — Judge Kess flipped when her niece Kara Stenn was freed), barge recruitment (Dalla Vask turned Vella Drace). Zero combat. Zero casualties. Blackveil arrested. Kess joined inter-city network.

Blackveil's ledger revealed: Crucible calibration fragment sent east to Fenhollow.

### Salthollow (Days 25–27)

Two days on the river, one day in Salthollow. Lorekeeper Vane's operation exposed: property acquisition, faculty purge, nightly document extraction. Allies recruited: Professor Dunwick, Dak, Councilwoman Ashworth, student Lira. Senna Vael turned by Orindel.

Sylvara discovered the **Athenaeum** — sealed pre-Sundering vault containing original civic charters for every town in the river basin, invalidating all network property seizures.

**Operation Athenaeum** — four phases at 11th bell: wagon interception (7 crates), Athenaeum opened (Ashworth's civic authority), archive third floor secured (Mira cracked strongbox, Sleight of Hand 21), civic trigger at dawn (Vane suspended, Senn arrested). Zero combat. Salthollow joined network.

### Outfitting & Fenhollow (Days 28–30)

Full gear audit at Thornfield waystation. Sylvara + Bram → chain shirts (AC 15). All party properly outfitted.

Fenhollow: occupied marsh town under Commander Ashford's curfew. Allies recruited: Captain Brenn (legitimate militia), Old Harren Fenn (marsh custodian), Sister Venn, Wynn Galley. Mira + Pip entered stockade via drainage grate — Mira lifted calibration fragment off Ashdale's desk (Sleight of Hand 24). Brenn reinstated at dawn. Ashdale's contact list revealed **Overseer Callista Venn** in Veldmere.

### Delta Run to Veldmere (Day 30–31)

Marsh punts overnight + Soren Blackwater's sloop *Marsh Hawk*. 18 hours Fenhollow to Veldmere, bypassing all checkpoints. Arrived public harbor, berth 14. Callista Venn in Sulwick's compound with iron chest.
