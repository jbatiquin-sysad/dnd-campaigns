### Edra Voss — Interrogation (Day 71)

**Identity:** Edra Voss, sister of Seren Voss (the missing Duskport archivist). Seren was killed — body found in Duskport harbor with ligature marks, 3 weeks after the Partition Writ.

**Motive:** Grief + survival. Contacted by the Watcher 6 months ago. Hired to establish Archive cover, identify barrow sites, recruit Veradis, manage logistics.

**The Watcher:** Communicates via Sending Stone (25 words/day). Old. Uses archaisms. Knows the Archive intimately — references internal procedures from decades ago. Afraid — urgent when delays reported. Believes the outer wards are failing.

**The sealed letter (opened in interrogation):** A second operative dispatched to Vel Tharis. Arriving 15th Brightmere. "Sethrak's Threshold failed last month. The cascade has begun."

**The Vigil of the Closed Eye:** Pre-Sundering ward-keeper order. Maintained the outer ward network. Believed destroyed during the Sundering. The closed eye symbol matches. The Watcher may be a surviving member.

**Edra turned over to Aelindra authorities.** Reward: 200 gp bounty + 100 gp expenses + permanent Archive access for Sylvara.

### Sending Stone — First Contact with the Watcher (Day 71)

Zaryth sent directly: *"This is Zaryth Vel'quinar, Dawnwatch. Edra is in custody. Veradis is in custody. We sealed Keth-Varos. If the wards are truly failing, we will help. Not with the dead. Talk to us."*

Watcher responded: *"You renewed the Root covenant. I watched. You are not fools. But peace will not hold the line when the wards break. Prove me wrong. Vel Tharis. Go see."*
