# Week 2 — The Road to Aelindra

**In-Game:** Day 2 (morning) through Day 9
**Session:** S3 Turns 1–10
**DM:** Aldric Voss, The Chronicler
**Party Level:** 1 → 2 (leveled Day 6, during voyage)

## Summary

An anonymous note led Zaryth to the Anchor & Crow, where Edric Aldenmere revealed the Vel Noctis were not hired by House Aldenmere — they were sent by the Archivist-General of Aelindra. The stolen Tome of Unraveling was connected to a larger conspiracy.

Edric disclosed: the seal on the Vel Noctis correspondence matched the Archivist-General's office. Sylvara recognized it immediately — and wrote one word: "Aelindra." Harwick provided the Vel Noctis contact name (Serevane) and confirmed a two-week-old trigger event: Serevane visited Duskport in person, met Lord Aldenmere, and may have identified Vael Orun as a counter-operative.

The party prepared for departure: ransomed captives (39gp now, 41gp on return), purchased equipment, sent Sylvara's letter to Veyra Solund via courier, and activated Serevane's iron disc (signaling their intent). Bram and Thessaly stayed to hold Duskport.

Four-day voyage to Aelindra. The party leveled to 2 en route. Truth or dare on Day 1. One-on-one conversations on Day 2. Training on Day 3. Rest and beer on Day 4. Serevane's reply arrived the night before departure: "I received your signal. I know who you are. Come to Aelindra. I will find you."
