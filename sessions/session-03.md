# Session 3 — The Archivist's Fire

**DM:** Aldric Voss, The Chronicler
**Date:** Session 3
**Turns:** 28 (S3 T1–T28)
**Party Level:** 1 → 2 (leveled during voyage to Aelindra)

## Summary

The party received an anonymous note leading them to Edric Aldenmere, who revealed the Vel Noctis were not hired by House Aldenmere — they were sent by the Archivist-General of Aelindra. The stolen Tome of Unraveling was connected to a larger conspiracy predating the Duskport operation.

The party traveled to Aelindra by ship (4 days), leveling to 2 en route. In Aelindra, they spent 6 days gathering intelligence while earning 256gp through cover jobs — courier work, Archive consulting, lamplighting, clinic healing, and merchant brokering.

Key discoveries: the Tome was not stolen but relocated by Archivist-General Thessara Kain to a hidden sub-level beneath the Crown. She intended to use it to unmake every ward, seal, and binding in the Archive simultaneously — collapsing the upper tiers of the city and releasing the contents of deep vaults sealed with blood magic. Estimated casualties: 5,000+ in the first hour.

Serevane, Thessara's former operative who ran the Duskport operation, defected and provided the party with maps, guard schedules, and a ward-key. Veyra Solund (Sylvara's mentor, senior archivist) committed to confronting Thessara directly.

On the night of Day 6, the party breached sub-level 2 during a guard changeover and executed the Valis Procedure — a controlled discharge of the Tome's stored energy through a four-anchor grounding circle. The procedure lasted 14 minutes (extended due to a 12-node crystal amplification array). All four anchors endured CON saves at DC 14. Sylvara carried Pip's collapsed quadrant for 19 seconds at the end. Orindel kept the anchors alive with two Healing Words and then six minutes of presence with no magic left.

Veyra Solund held Thessara in conversation for the entire procedure, keeping her calm and the emotional ward open. Thessara chose not to intervene. When it was over, she chose redemption — testified before the senior council and accepted house arrest.

The Tome was returned to the primary vault under a collective key. Veyra was appointed interim Archivist-General. Serevane disappeared. Edric stayed in Aelindra to assist with Archive reform.

The party sailed home to Duskport over 4 days, sharing dreams, aspirations, and darkest hours. They arrived to find Thessaly and Bram had held the city — captives secure, Voss inquiry proceeding, Calder quiet.
