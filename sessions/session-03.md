## Summary (so far)

Session 3 opened the morning after the council victory. A mysterious note arrived — "The Vel Noctis did not originate in Duskport. They were sent here." The sender turned out to be Edric Aldenmere, who revealed that House Aldenmere was a client, not the principal. The Vel Noctis answer to the Archivist-General of Aelindra.

Harwick's full testimony (broken under combined Intimidation 23 from Zaryth and Mira) named the Vel Noctis contact: Serevane — a codename, not a real name, an official function of the Archivist-General's office. The broken chain seal was identified as the mark of the Unchained Compact, a sub-order within Aelindra whose motto is "What is sealed shall open."

Vael Orun revealed the Unchained Compact targets constraints — not to possess them, but to remove them. The Partition Writ was practice. The Tome of Unraveling is the point.

The party signaled Serevane via the iron disc, earned 95gp through side work, equipped for the journey, levelled to 2, and sailed to Aelindra over four days of bonding, training, and beer.

In Aelindra, the party established a safehouse (the Inkwell), scouted the city, tested Sylvara's credentials (inactive, not revoked — levels 1-3 access), and contacted Veyra Solund. Veyra revealed the Archivist-General likely staged the Tome's theft and has been relocating restricted documents for 18 months.

The Writ of Inquiry can be filed in 2 days. Serevane is circling — her watcher made eye contact with Zaryth and walked away.
