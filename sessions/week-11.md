Zaryth received the activation sequence from Vaelith with reverence, handed it to Sylvara. Party entered Greyveil Pass in formation. Entities massed on both ridgelines — dozens of shapes, watching.

**Zaryth addressed the entities directly** — assured them the Dawnwatch was here to repair the boundary, not fight. The entities yielded ground on both ridgelines. Vaelith: "No one has spoken to them in a thousand years."

**Warden's Vault opened.** Retrieved: resonance crystal (pre-tuned to Node 12), ritual components, and the **Vigil Field Manual** — complete ward-engineering document for all 14 nodes.

Vaelith wept. Named the Dawnwatch as wardens of the Vigil.

### Warden's Forge (Days 109–110)

Two days north of Greyveil on the Vigil road. Stone building built into mountainside. Self-sustaining forge-ward has burned for 1,000+ years.

**Node 11 resonance crystal cut and tuned.** Sylvara cutting, Orindel listening (ear for ward frequency), Vaelith guiding. Full day of work.

**8x Dawnwatch rings enchanted.** Bram worked the forge. Each ring heated to resonance temperature, quenched in ward-water. Properties: ward-network recognition, passage through all ward lines, resonance pulse (danger awareness between bearers — warmth in metal, unlimited range within network).

Raw resonance stone remaining: 2.5 blocks (stored at forge for future use).

### Node 11 — Sethrak's Threshold (Days 111–112)

Two days northeast from the forge. Beyond the ward line. Necrotic saturation. Dead ground.

**Pip trained flute on the road** — five-note scale mastered, full hymn melody learned under Orindel's coaching.

**Opposition: 14 ward-wraiths** — inverted Vigil wardens in Final Stand formation. Not mindless — remembered their duty. Had been guarding the dead node for 14 months.

**Zaryth approached alone, unarmed.** Communicated with the wraith leader. Showed the enchanted ring (ward frequency). Sylvara showed the crystal. The wraiths recognized the note. Leader ordered stand-down: *"...let them pass... the singing... comes back..."* **All 14 wraiths yielded. Zero combat.**

**Node 11 reconstructed** — 6-anchor procedure, 20 minutes. Zaryth as **Warden's Anchor** (CHA-based, non-caster channeling through conviction). CON saves DC 15: Pip failed at minute 5 (Orindel Healing Word), Sylvara failed at minute 12 (Vaelith guided correction), Aldric failed at minute 12 (Orindel's last Healing Word), Orindel failed at minute 12 — **sang the Ward-Light Hymn to hold his anchor** (channeled ward frequency through music, zero spell slots remaining). Pip failed at minute 18 — self-recovered with flute. Zaryth passed all 3 CHA checks (22, 19, 16).

**Node 11: FAILED → 75%.** Golden light column. Sky torn open. 14 ward-wraiths dissolved into motes of light — went home. Leader's last words: *"I can hear it. Thank you, Warden."*
