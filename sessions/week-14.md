### World History Session

Vaelith and Seren spent two hours telling the full history of the known world to the assembled party:

- **Age of Binding** (~3000–1500 BS) — The Binders raised the first ward network
- **Covenant Kingdoms** (~1500–800 BS) — Five kingdoms (Aelindros, Vel Tharion, Keth Morath, Ashenmere, Stormcape) signed the Covenant of Binding
- **Fracture Wars** (~800–200 BS) — Covenant collapsed, nodes weaponized, Vigil hunted
- **The Sundering** (Year 0) — Unchained Compact faction shattered the ward network at the Root. Three days of catastrophe. Root pulsed as failsafe.
- **The Silence** (1–200 AS) — Scattered survival. Hearthcoast cities founded. Vigil worked in secret.
- **Reconstruction through Modern Age** — Archive rose, pattern repeated, Thessara Kain, the Dawnwatch.

### Vaelith's Resonance Bond

Vaelith revealed how he survived 1,000+ years. During the Sundering, the Root's emergency pulse passed through him while his hands were on Node 12's stone. The ward frequency fused into his body — he became a living conduit. As long as the ward network exists, he exists. When nodes degrade, he feels physical pain. When the Dawnwatch repaired nodes, the pain stopped.

Faint root-like lines visible on his wrist radiating from the closed-eye mark. Seren confirmed: field logs reference "resonance-bonded keeper" as a theoretical Binder failsafe. Vaelith is the only known instance.
