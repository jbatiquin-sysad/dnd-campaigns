### The Sealed Quorum — Identified and Dissolved (Days 36–38)

Thessara Kain provided the four names: Archivist Dorin Kael (Internal Review, enforcer), Archivist Seren Voss (Collections, fled), Archivist Pell Maren (External Relations, fled with 6 amplifiers), Archivist Lenne Duvane (Ward Maintenance, wavering).

- **Duvane turned** at the Chapel of the Quiet Archive (Zaryth Persuasion 20, Aldric Persuasion 19). Provided master calibration index. Agreed to testify.
- **Serevane's encryption key** + Veyra's mail access = 247 Quorum messages decrypted. All 4 identities confirmed. Phase 3 plan exposed: simultaneous activation of all sealed sites.
- **Kael suspended** by Veyra. Office sealed. Contingencies found by Mira: ward-solvent vial (could dissolve Root seal), escape plan (*Grey Fin*), back-door map to the Root.
- **Maren interdicted** by Kess + Torben Vask + 20 miners at Greymoor cove. All 6 amplifiers secured. Maren arrested.
- **Voss** missing but defanged — no calibration index, no amplifiers, no network.

**Dawnwatch signet rings** forged by Master Orin Ashvale from 2 steel ingots. Distributed by Zaryth. Pip cried.

**Thessara's Public Testimony (Day 38):** Senior archivist council chamber, open session. Thessaly presented evidence. Duvane testified. Sylvara authenticated correspondence. Thessara spoke last. Archivist Brenn Holt reversed position: "This session is not the wound. It is the surgery." All resolutions passed **11-0**: Quorum dissolved, charges authorized, reform mandate, Archivist-General authorized to secure all sealed sites.

**7/7 targets handled.** Thessaly drew the seventh line.

### The Root (Day 39, Dawn)

Sylvara discovered the full covenant text: *"The Root sleeps. The Archive watches. This is the covenant. When the watchers number eight and the chain is broken, the Root may be asked — not commanded — to wake."*

Veyra and Thessara both approved the approach. Sylvara taught the party pre-Sundering history on the Inkwell rooftop — the Wellsprings, the Sundering, the Sealing, the Covenant.

At dawn, the Dawnwatch descended to the Root seal — a 12-foot circular stone door covered in 200-year-old ward-marks. Zaryth placed his ring hand on the seal and spoke: asking, not commanding, if the Root was ready to wake.

**The Root responded.** Golden light spread from the seal in eight rays matching the Dawnwatch insignia. The Wellspring woke. Ward-marks across the entire continental network flared gold simultaneously — in Duskport, Greymoor, Salthollow, Fenhollow, Veldmere. Every sealed site strengthened. The covenant was renewed.

The ward network is now actively maintained by a conscious Wellspring. The convergence plan is permanently dead.

### Dawnwatch Homestead (Days 40–45)

The party sailed south and purchased 4 acres on Fenhollow's inland rise (120gp, Wynn Galley's arrangement with Maren Blackthorn). Stone cottage, spring-fed stream, 10 fruit trees, wild bee colonies.

**8 days of building:**
- **Roof:** Bram + Aldric + Zaryth (Bram shaped beams, Zaryth thatched, Wynn taught him)
- **Hearth:** Aldric solo (river stones, dwarven technique, 1 steel ingot for iron grate)
- **Garden:** Mira + Orindel (herbs, vegetables, flowers — Mira planted every seed herself)
- **Beehives:** Orindel + Pip (3 colonies transferred, Pip stung 11 times Day 2)
- **Orchard:** Sylvara + Thessaly (pruned 10 trees, treated fungal issue)
- **Camping ground:** Bram + Zaryth (4 platforms, fire pit, latrine)
- **Interior:** Everyone (table for 10, 4 rooms, kitchen, writing desk for Sylvara)

**Caretaker hired:** Tessa Marsh (Corwin Marsh's sister, widowed) + Ren (12) + Lila (9). 4gp/month + loft room. Tessa knows bees, gardens, orchards. Ren wants to learn magic from Pip. Lila adopted Mira's herb garden.

First supper at the homestead: Mira cooked. Vegetable stew from the first garden greens. Orindel placed the carved Marsh Light icon on the mantelpiece. "For the house."
