# Week 9 — The Ward Cascade

**In-Game:** Days 69–80
**Session:** S5 (continued)
**DM:** Aldric Voss, The Chronicler
**Party Level:** 3

## Summary

### Vel Tharis — Cleared and Sealed (Days 73–75)

Party scouted the coastal cliffs north of Aelindra. Mira mapped the sea cave entrance (low tide access, 6-hour window). Sylvara measured **Node 9 with the Ward Compass: 40% capacity, degrading.**

Standing stone with closed eye symbol on the cliff top confirmed Vigil presence.

**Day 75 — Intercept:** Second operative arrived. A mercenary (Veteran stat block), not a caster — hired in Duskport with a pre-made catalyst kit and phonetic activation card. Zaryth called for surrender. Operative attacked. Combat: 2 rounds, party took 11 damage (Zaryth only), operative dropped to 0 HP. Subdued, bound, interrogated.

Key finding: the Watcher has reduced the catalyst method to mass-producible kits. Any literate person can execute it.

Sea cave entrance collapsed and sealed (Aldric masonry + Sylvara Arcane Lock). **Vel Tharis: SEALED (2/7).**

Operative turned over to Aelindra authorities. Reward: 100 gp.

### Ward Cascade — Confirmed (Days 75–76)

Sylvara's Ward Compass reading at Vel Tharis confirmed the outer ward network is real and degrading. Zaryth sent via Sending Stone: *"We measured Node 9. Forty percent. We understand the cascade. We want to repair the wards, not raise the dead. Share what you know. We can fix this."*

Watcher responded with node statuses: **Node 10 at 31%. Node 12 at 28%.** Worse than projected.

Sylvara's research (Aelindra Archive, Level 3 restricted access):
- 14 outer ward anchor nodes forming continental perimeter
- Pre-Sundering construction, maintained by the Vigil of the Closed Eye
- No maintenance in 1,247 years
- Cascade model: Node 12 fails in ~2 months, full perimeter collapse in 6–8 months
- Ward repair ritual reconstruction: 90% complete — missing the activation sequence
- Vigil caches documented near Nodes 9, 12, and 14 — emergency repair kits with resonance crystals

### The Watcher's Condition (Day 77)

Zaryth asked what the Watcher wants in exchange for sharing knowledge.

Response: *"Meet me. In person. Node 12. The northern pass called Greyveil. I will be there on the first of Highsun. Come alone or with your party. But come."*

Follow-up exchange confirmed: the Watcher placed the Greyveil cache personally. They have the complete repair ritual — the Archive version is incomplete. Sylvara needs the Watcher's portion.

### Return to Homestead (Days 77–80)

Party returned to Fenhollow by charter carriage. Sylvara remained in Aelindra for continued research. Full homestead operations resumed.
