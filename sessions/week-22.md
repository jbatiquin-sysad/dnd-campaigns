**Title:** Vel'tarn Rising / Vel'mora First Contact

## Summary

The Chronicler took the helm for the most expansive session in campaign history — spanning over a year of in-game time across two continents. The Dawnwatch named their village Vel'tarn ("those who choose peace"), placed the World's Hearthstone, deployed independent planting teams, expanded trade operations, upgraded the Tidecaller, crossed 2,800 miles of ocean, and made first contact with all five races of the western continent Vel'mora. The kah-sol — an 800-year-old alien network intelligence in the Deep Forest — was revealed to be lonely, not malicious. The solution is a seed, not a sword. Level 7 achieved through 5/5 Ascended profession training across all five races.
