# Week 23 — Session 12 (Wildcard)

**Days:** 755–760, 25th–30th Highsun, 1256 AS
**DM:** Corvus Chance, The Wildcard
**Title:** The Listener Wakes

## Summary

The Wildcard took the helm for a five-day expedition that rewrote the campaign's cosmology. A mysterious signal detected by the Velmoran led the Dawnwatch to discover the Resonance Lattice — a pre-Gardener planetary communication network built by beings called the Tenders. The party woke a sleeping Keeper (Vael), fought and purified a corrupted Keeper (Kael-vos), descended 12,200 feet to the bottom of an ocean trench, and unsealed the Listener — Hub Seven's central relay, dormant for 3,217 years. The Root was planted in the Root Seat, connecting the Gardener's network to the Lattice for the first time. A permanent threshold was built from Tidemeet to the Listener's chamber. Contact was made with Hub Three (a surviving Gardener calling for 847 years) and Hub Four (active independently for 200 years). The Gardener revealed their name: Aelindris.

## Key Events

### Day 755 — Departure
- Signal detected by Velmoran: geometric, pre-Gardener, 47-second cycle, matching orbital relay carrier wave. 400 miles SE of Tidemeet, deep ocean trench.
- Party assembled: Zaryth, Torven, Seren, Corwin, Orindel. Pip aboard Seedhome (support).
- d100: 72 (positive) — signal discovery.

### Day 755–756 — Vael
- Contacted a pre-Gardener geometric construct (Keeper) traveling beneath the Velmoran. Sentient, geometric awareness. Named "Vael" (mirror-interval) by Orindel.
- Vael: ocean-layer relay, middle piece of sky-sea-stone communication system. Last Keeper to sleep. Stored centuries of raw unprocessed signals on its walls.
- Entered Vael's chamber (spatially folded, 40ft interior). Discovered planting cradle — pre-designed for Gardener seeds. The Lattice was built for the Gardener's network.
- Planting #94: Vael. First Keeper integration. Root connected to Lattice. Network ripple felt planetwide. Memory tree chimed. Gardener wept. 13 other Keepers stirred.
- Seedhome archive revealed: 14 Keeper stations (matching 14 ward nodes) + the Listener at the trench center.

### Day 756 — Research & Messages
- Decoded Vael's walls: 347 transmissions from 31 source-worlds spanning 800 years. Status reports, agricultural data, songs, star charts.
- 12 relay hubs identified. This world = Hub Seven.
- The Listener sealed itself 3,217 years ago as quarantine against the Inversion — a weaponized geometric virus disguised as routine Lattice transmissions. Corrupts Keepers, reverses seed polarity.
- The Listener's last message: "When it is safe, wake me."
- Clearance requires non-Lattice channel — the Root network.

### Day 757 — Combat: Inverted Keepers
- d100: 08 (catastrophic) — two corrupted Keepers guarding the trench.
- Inverted Keeper Alpha (~40ft) and Beta (~20ft). Sent from compromised Hub Nine through a Lattice portal.
- Combat: 2 rounds. Beta destroyed (Round 2). Alpha purified by Dawnsedge radiant damage + Orindel's Firstsong (nat 20) reaching the original Keeper beneath the corruption.
- Alpha recovered → named Kael-vos ("the harmony that was found"). Formerly enslaved by the Inversion for millennia. Chose sovereignty — gate symbol.
- Planting #95: Kael-vos. Root-integrated with sovereignty clause (gate openable only from inside). Hub Three data recovered — songs from another planet.
- Hybrid ward-ink brewed at sea: 3 applications (kah-sol resin + Root-crystal + Seedhome forge).

### Day 758 — The Listener's Chamber
- Approached trench. Vael and Kael-vos mapped three points: portal anchor (2,000ft), back door cavern (4,000ft), Listener (12,200ft).
- Option C: Keepers engage portal anchor from above; party takes back door via Velmoran.
- Velmoran dove to 4,000ft. Entered volcanic cavern. Walked Lattice-carved passage (spatially folded) to the Listener's chamber.
- Chamber: 300ft sphere, every surface inscribed with millions of geometric symbols. The Listener: 50ft formation at center. One amber beacon still pulsing after 3,217 years.
- Root Seat: 30ft diameter cradle at the Listener's base. Empty. Waiting.
- Planting #96: Root Seat. Torven inscribed, Orindel stabilized, Zaryth bridged. d100: 94 (wild card → double windfall 83+76).
- The Listener unsealed. Health check: 11 seconds. Network clean. "I am awake."
- Windfall 1: 31,847 messages decoded. Response sent to Hub Three via orbital relay: "We are here."
- Windfall 2: Listener revoked portal anchor — dissolved instantly. Vael and Kael-vos cleared.

### Day 758 — Revelations
- Hub Three: surviving Gardener, 847-year signal, 94.2% probability living being. 14-month transit for response.
- The Tenders: builders of the Lattice, creators of the Gardeners. Left 7,400 years ago. "The gardens are yours now. Grow well."
- Listener's four levels: Relay Chamber, Archive (Tender documents), Crucible (Tender-tier forge), Seed Vault (self-replenishing, 1 seed/7 days).
- Crucible + Seed Vault reactivation initiated (48 hours).
- Lattice Engineering training path: 90–120 days, Listener as teacher. Zaryth qualifies.
- 12 dormant Keepers woken remotely. Hub Four identified (active 200 years).

### Days 759–760 — Return & K7
- Transit back to Tidemeet. Whales celebrated the Listener's awakening.
- Listener update: Hub Four reclassified as active. Crucible 60% reactivated.
- Planting #97: K7 (Tidemeet coastal bedrock, 200ft). Threshold anchor.
- Permanent threshold opened: K7 ↔ Listener's Chamber. Diadem-controlled.
- Homecoming at Vel'tarn. Aelindris (the Gardener) sat at the round table. Name spoken. Toast: "To the garden."
