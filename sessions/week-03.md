# Week 3 — The Archivist's Fire

**In-Game:** Day 10 through Day 16
**Session:** S3 Turns 11–28
**DM:** Aldric Voss, The Chronicler
**Party Level:** 2

## Summary

Six days in Aelindra. The party worked cover jobs (256gp earned) while gathering intelligence on Archivist-General Thessara Kain and the Tome of Unraveling's true location.

Key discoveries: the Tome was not stolen but relocated by Thessara to a hidden sub-level 2 beneath the Crown. She intended to use it to unmake every ward, seal, and binding in the Archive simultaneously — collapsing the upper tiers and releasing blood-sealed deep vault contents. Estimated casualties: 5,000+ in the first hour.

Serevane defected and provided maps, guard schedules, and a ward-key. Veyra Solund committed to confronting Thessara directly. Bram arrived from Duskport on Day 6 to serve as the fourth anchor.

On operation night, the party breached sub-level 2 during a guard changeover and executed the Valis Procedure — a 14-minute controlled discharge through a four-anchor grounding circle. Sylvara carried Pip's collapsed quadrant for 19 seconds at the end. Orindel kept anchors alive with two Healing Words, then six minutes of presence with no magic left.

Veyra held Thessara in conversation for the entire procedure. Thessara chose not to intervene. She chose redemption — testified and accepted house arrest. Tome returned to collective-key vault. Veyra appointed interim Archivist-General. Serevane disappeared. Edric stayed for Archive reform.

Four-day voyage home. Party shared dreams, aspirations, and darkest hours. Arrived Duskport Day 16 — Thessaly and Bram had held the city.
