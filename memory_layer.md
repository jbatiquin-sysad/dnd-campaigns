## Campaign Summary (Sessions 1–4) — Archived

> Full turn-by-turn logs: `memory_archive_s1-s4.md`

### Session 1 (DM: Chronicler) — The Partition Writ
Pip's 200gp debt to the Crimson Ledger led to discovering the Vel Noctis — a pre-Sundering guild using debts as leverage. Party traced the conspiracy to the Undercroft beneath Duskport, rescued Maret Soll (2 months captive) and recovered the Partition Writ (Third Compact civic document protecting the harbor district from private seizure). House Aldenmere exposed as the principal. Zero casualties.

### Session 2 (DM: Storyteller) — The Reckoning
Party split: Group A delivered the Writ to Magistrate Sera Voss, Group B extracted Lord Edric Aldenmere (secret ally, testified against his own family) and Harwick (family solicitor, revealed as Vael Orun's 10-year double agent). Edric's nat 20 testimony broke the council session. House Aldenmere harbor acquisitions frozen. Calder neutralized at the door.

### Session 3 (DM: Chronicler) — Aelindra
Edric revealed Vel Noctis answered to the Archivist-General of Aelindra. Party traveled to Aelindra, discovered Serevane (defected Vel Noctis contact), located the Tome of Unraveling in a secret sub-level 2 beneath the Crown. Executed the Valis Procedure (14 minutes, 4 anchors) to discharge the Tome. Thessara Kain chose redemption. Veyra Solund appointed interim Archivist-General. Party leveled to 2. Edric stayed in Aelindra for reform.

### Session 4 (DM: Wildcard → Storyteller) — The Six Cities & The Root
Calder's coordinated strike on return (d100=07 catastrophic). Temple fire suppressed, Voss assassination prevented, captives recaptured, Calder arrested. Then: Harrowgate (Tetch/Corvath — funding severed), Greymoor (Pallid Court/Blackveil — Crucible secured), Salthollow (Vane/Senn — Athenaeum charters recovered), Fenhollow (Ashford/Ashdale — wardhouse secured), Veldmere (Sulwick/Dray/Venn — Shard of Unmaking neutralized, Silver Chain allied). Aelindra: Sealed Quorum dissolved. **7/7 targets complete.** The Root awakened — continental ward covenant renewed. Party leveled to 3. Dawnwatch named. Homestead purchased and built.
