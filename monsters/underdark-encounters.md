## Special / Scripted Encounters

For key story moments. DM places these, not rolled randomly.

### The Rust Monster Nest (Any Zone)
**1× Rust Monster** (CR ½). Antennae corrode nonmagical metal on touch. Drawn to metal — Zaryth and Aldric are walking buffets. **Tactical warning:** party has enchanted weapons (+1 magical, immune to rust). But shields, spare tools, pitons, and lanterns are vulnerable. Mira's thieves' tools are metal. Tension without catastrophe.

### The Aboleth's Domain (Deep — Zone 3+)
**1× Aboleth** (CR 10). **DEADLY — do not use casually.** Underground lake. Telepathic contact first — probes desires, offers bargains. Mucus Cloud underwater. Enslave (DC 14 WIS). This is a negotiation encounter that *becomes* combat if diplomacy fails. The Aboleth may know about House Vel'quinar — it remembers everything. It has been here since before the Sundering.

### Drow Ambush — House Patrol
**4× Drow** + **1× Drider** (CR ~7 total). Sleep-poison crossbow bolts. Darkness spell. Web traps. They want prisoners, not corpses. If Zaryth is recognized as Drow, the dynamic shifts — interrogation, not execution. If his house sigil is seen, it shifts again. This encounter is story, not just combat.

## Party Considerations

**Strengths:**
- Action economy (5 turns/round)
- 2 clerics (healing depth)
- 3 evocation wizards (Sculpt Spells — safe AoE in tunnels)
- All weapons enchanted (+1 magical — bypasses nonmagical resistance)
- Zaryth: Darkvision 120 ft, speaks Elvish/Undercommon, Underdark navigation skills
- Alchemy stores: antitoxin, water breathing, fire/lightning/necrotic resistance potions

**Weaknesses:**
- Wizard HP (14–15) — one good hit drops them
- Halflings have no darkvision (Mira, Thessaly, Pip) — depend on light sources
- Heavy armor (Zaryth, Aldric) = loud, disadvantage on Stealth, vulnerable to Rust Monster
- Sunlight Sensitivity (Zaryth) — not relevant underground, but Faerie Fire from Drow enemies inverts his advantage
- No ranger in the default party comp (Seren stays home unless selected)

**Recommended Expedition Comp:**
Zaryth (Paladin — tank, Underdark knowledge, Darkvision 120 ft) + Mira (Rogue — scout, traps) + Aldric (Cleric — heals, Turn Undead) + Orindel (Cleric — backup heals, ranged) + Torven (Druid — Wild Shape scout, Nature checks, Darkvision 60 ft)

*Alt comp for stealth:* Zaryth + Mira + Thessaly + Pip + Seren (Monk — speed, ki, Darkvision via potion)
