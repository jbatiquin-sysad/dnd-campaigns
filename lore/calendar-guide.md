## Seasonal Effects

### Spring (Greenthaw – Highsun, Days 1–90)
- Roads muddy early, improve by Highsun
- Rivers high — barge travel fast but unpredictable
- Herb growth begins mid-Brightmere
- Planting window: Greenthaw through Brightmere

### Summer (Solstice – Reaptide, Days 91–180)
- Peak travel season — all roads passable
- Best foraging (Goldmere especially)
- Griffon breeding in Greypeaks (territorial, avoid high crags)
- Sea travel safest
- Harvest begins late Goldmere, peaks in Reaptide

### Autumn (Ashfall – Greymist, Days 181–240)
- Leaves turn, temperatures drop
- Smoking/preserving season (Ashfall)
- Last reliable trade caravans depart by mid-Greymist
- Fog and rain increase — visibility drops, marsh travel hazardous
- Sea storms begin late Greymist

### Winter (Frostmere – Longnight, Days 241–330)
- Snow in Greypeaks by Frostmere, lowlands by Deepwinter
- Most overland roads impassable Deepwinter–Longnight
- Rivers freeze (Mirren freezes mid-Deepwinter)
- Sea travel dangerous — storms, ice
- Settlements rely on stored provisions
- Fenhollow Marshes freeze partially — different travel routes open on ice
- The Dead Zones beyond the wards grow more active in winter (longer nights, thinner boundary)

### Pre-Spring (Thawsend, Days 331–360)
- Ice cracks, slow thaw
- Earliest planting possible late Thawsend
- Roads begin to clear
- Trade planning for the new year

## Moon Phases

The world has one moon — **Selara** ("the Silver Eye"). Its cycle is 30 days, perfectly aligned with the calendar months. Each month begins with the New Moon and ends with the Waning Crescent.

### Monthly Moon Cycle

| Day of Month | Phase | Illumination | Notes |
|-------------|-------|-------------|-------|
| 1–3 | **New Moon** | 0% | Darkest nights. Drow surface activity safest. Underdark rituals. |
| 4–7 | **Waxing Crescent** | 1–25% | First sliver visible at sunset. |
| 8–11 | **First Quarter** | 25–50% | Half-moon. Moderate light. |
| 12–14 | **Waxing Gibbous** | 50–75% | Bright evenings. |
| 15–17 | **Full Moon** | 75–100% | Brightest nights. Eilistraee holy days. Lycanthrope activity peaks. Priestess Sera's rituals strongest. |
| 18–21 | **Waning Gibbous** | 75–50% | Still bright. |
| 22–25 | **Last Quarter** | 50–25% | Half-moon, visible at dawn. |
| 26–29 | **Waning Crescent** | 25–1% | Fading. |
| 30 | **Dark Moon** | 0% | Last night of the month. Traditionally a night of reflection. |

### Moon Phase Effects

| Effect | Phase | Mechanic |
|--------|-------|----------|
| **Eilistraee's Blessing** | Full Moon (15th–17th) | Priestess Sera's divine magic at full strength. Eilistraee rituals (including memory restoration) most effective. |
| **Drow Sunlight Relief** | New Moon (1st–3rd) | Darkest nights — Drow can operate on the surface without Sunlight Sensitivity penalties (no sun, minimal moonlight). |
| **Lycanthrope Risk** | Full Moon (15th–17th) | Shapechangers most active. Relevant for wilderness travel. |
| **Ward Resonance** | Full Moon (15th–17th) | Ward network readings are slightly stronger during full moon — the Binding frequency resonates with lunar tidal forces. Vaelith: "The moon pulls the boundary the way it pulls the sea." |
| **Divination** | New Moon (1st–3rd) | Divination magic slightly more reliable (less ambient magical interference). |
| **Underdark Tides** | Full Moon (15th–17th) | Underground water levels rise slightly (lunar tidal effect on aquifers). The Wet Section on the Deep Road floods more during full moons. |

### Key Lunar Dates (Campaign Relevance)

| Date | Moon Phase | Significance |
|------|-----------|-------------|
| 15th of any month | Full Moon | Sera's ritual window for Zaryth's memory restoration |
| 1st–3rd of any month | New Moon | Best window for Drow surface operations / Vel'quinar integration |
| 15th Solstice (Midsummer) | Full Moon + longest day | Strongest ward resonance of the year |
| 15th Longnight | Full Moon + shortest day | Full moon on the darkest month — Eilistraee's most sacred night |
